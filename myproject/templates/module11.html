--Mental Health & Well-Being: The Need Of The Hour--



Mental health is a vital and necessary component of health. As per World Health Organisation (WHO), mental health is not just the absence of mental disorders or disabilities, it is more than that. Mental health is a state of wellness in which an individual recognises his or her own abilities, adapts coping with the regular stresses of life and can work productively. Hence, it is high time that we focus on increasing and creating awareness about the need to take care of mental health issues










  Types of Mental Health issues

Depression

Depression is one of the most common mental health issues. It generally involves having consistent low mood and lack of interest in daily activities. Depression has a negative impact on how you feel, your thinking and reaction.

Anxiety Disorders

Anxiety disorder is a kind of mental health condition wherein you may respond to certain things and situations with fear. You may also experience physical signs as a increase in heart beat and sweating.

Stress Management

A feeling of emotional or physical tension triggers stress. It can occur due to any event or thought that makes you feel upset, angry, or nervous. Stress is your body's reaction to a challenge or demand.
